NAVER App Tracking SDK Integration Guide

Notice

Support for the App Conversion Tracking SDK will be discontinued starting Thursday, August 21, 2025.
Advertisers currently using the SDK are advised to remove it and explore alternative solutions—such as integrating with MMP(Mobile Measurement Partner)—before and after the shutdown.
For further details, please refer to the official notice sent via email.

NAVER App Tracking SDK (hereinafter, “the SDK”) helps you collect NAVER app conversion events. This document describes how to integrate the SDK into your iOS or Android app. For more information about how to sign up for NAVER Performance Display Ad (DA) and add ads, see "NAVER Performance DA user guide" in NAVER Performance DA platform notice.
